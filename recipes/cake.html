Jorblet Cake

What's this all about?

This is forbidden desert
You will not be forgiven for cooking this.

How we do this

Prep time: The ritual takes 1D4 hours.
Cook time: Instantaneous

  • 2 C flour
  • 2 regular fowl eggs
  • 1 Qt Stevia
  • 1 Eridani Jorblet, traumatized
  1. In a medium bowl whisk together flour, eggs, and Stevia.
  2. Cry apologies to the gods
  3. Perform the ritual
